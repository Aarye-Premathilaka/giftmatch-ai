How it works

A simple three-step process to make gift searching easier and more creative.

1

Describe the person

Write a few details about the person, such as age, hobbies, personality, and interests.

2

Choose smart filters

Select relationship, budget up to CHF 15'000, age group, interests, and country.

3

See matches and links

Get gift ideas with match percentages, then browse shopping-style result cards.

Why Giftmatch.ai?

Finding gifts can be stressful. Giftmatch.ai makes the process simple, fun, safe, and creative.

Personal ideas

Suggestions are based on the person’s interests, not just random products.

Teen-friendly

The design is simple and easy for students, teenagers, and families to use.

No login needed

The website is static, fast, free to host, and does not require accounts or passwords.

Safe shopping

Giftmatch.ai shows trusted shopping examples and avoids unsafe or suspicious shops.

Trusted examples

Links may point to Amazon, Galaxus, Digitec, Migros, Coop, Manor, Smyths Toys, Ochsner Sport, Sports Direct, Argos, Target, Walmart, Best Buy, Decathlon, Fnac, Bol, and similar trusted shops.

Unsafe shops avoided

The website avoids Temu, AliExpress, fake shops, illegal shops, or suspicious websites.

Always check yourself

Prices, availability, delivery, safety, and reviews should always be checked before buying.

About

My name is Aarye Premathilaka. I am 14 years old and I live in Zug, Switzerland. Giftmatch.ai is my creative student project. The idea came from a real problem: sometimes it is difficult to find the right gift for someone, even when you know that person well.

This website was created with the help of AI. I designed the idea, wrote the prompts, improved the features, and guided the project step by step. The style is inspired by clean and modern Google-like product design, but Giftmatch.ai is not an official Google product.

I am very interested in technology, problem solving, and application development. My goal is to get a Lehrstelle in 2027 as an Informatiker Applikationsentwickler at Google Schweiz GmbH. With this project, I want to show my motivation, creativity, and ability to build something useful from an idea.

I am thankful to Google for inspiring many young people like me with useful products, smart ideas, and modern technology. I hope Giftmatch.ai shows that I am serious, curious, and ready to keep learning.
